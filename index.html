About Me

I was born in france and came to israel in 2006. I always was pasionate about videogames and always dream on creating my own videogames.

Eventually I joined The Nahal Brigade of the IDF for my 3 Years military service, I evolve from a common soldier to commander and finnaly to sergeant with 50 soldier under my responsability.This experience gave me a lot of new skills such as Leadership, Work under duress, Team Work, Managment and Strategic thinking.
When I quit the army and after a well deserved year off I started working as a security officer in an hotel, it was a student job that allowed me to pay for my computer science degree. The most valuable skill I picked up from that experience was probably Customer Service as I had to deal with a lot of angry and happy customer during those 5 years ...
When the corona started in 2020 i was let go of that job and no longer had the money to pay for my degree, I had to stop university to find a job and I tried on my own to keep learning programing and find alternate solutions to university.
